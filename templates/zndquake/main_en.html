A new disaster prevention experience

"Zunda Quake" is a software that receives disaster prevention information such as earthquakes and tsunamis in Japan and the world,*1 in real time, and notifies you by display and voice on the screen.

Key Features

Real-Time Notification

Receive the Earthquake Early Warning and tsunami information almost without delay, and immediately display it on the screen and notify you by voice.

Modern UI

A simple design with a green tone for easy viewing. It is a colour scheme that is gentle to the eyes even in the dark.

Multi-Platform

Support for Windows, Mac, and Linux*2, so it works regardless of your environment.
